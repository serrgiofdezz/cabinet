This is an important notice for users with a Google Worspace account under BBJProjeK (bbjprojek.org) domain;

As of September 1st, 2024, we are gonna upgrade our plan from "Business Starter" to "Business Plus", with no additional cost for current users. This upgrade to accounts with Workspace include;

* 5 TB pooled storage per user
* 500 participant video meetings plus recording
* Email layouts and mail merge
* Vault access and advanced endpoint management

--------------

If any user want to upgrade from his regular bbjprojek.org account to a Google Workspace one, contact to the team at hello@bbjprojek.org, explaining why would you want migrate to a Google Workspace and your usage, alongside with your current bbjprojek.org email and other information. Remember that upgrading your account has some limitations on our services, such as accessing our own email platform, as you now would be using Google Workspace one (Gmail).

If you need more information, just contact us. Enjoy the upgrade!

        https://admin.google.com/domain?=bbjprojek.org
