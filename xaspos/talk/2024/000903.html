Starting on September 23th, Ragyxo will use the main BBJProjeK Mail Infrastructure. This mean that users registered with a @ragyxo.com account will have to login through BBJProjeK Mail website.

Ragyxo users now have access to some features and services of BBJProjeK, such as BBJProjeK Drive, BBJProjeK XasPos (read-only), and the Playground.

This merge has been approved and specified in the 50/2024 Internal RaBBAu Note. To find the content of this note, go to the RaBBAu Notes Finder.
