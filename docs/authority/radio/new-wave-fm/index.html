New Wave FM

New Wave FM is a radio station that plays "new wave" and latin spanish urban anthems.

  • Genre: Latin Urban. New Wave

Prominent Appearances

Trivia

  • This radio setlist was created back in 2021, known as "descanso en poder", just how the radio icon shows.
  • New Wave FM's DJ persona, known as El Cronista del Cambio, often delves into the historical significance of the new wave movement while drawing parallels with the evolution of Latin Spanish urban music. This educational aspect enhances the listener's experience, offering a deeper understanding of the cultural influences shaping the station's eclectic playlist.
